What is the minimum duration for investment?

Due to the nature of the underlying loans' terms, our notes require a minimum holding duration of 6 months.

What is the minimum investment amount?

We will accept a minimum investment of $1000, however we are looking to establish long term relationships to continually invest significantly larger amounts.

For what duration will the returns be maintained?

Returns to our investors will be scaled according to the local market conditions in which our flippers operate. Returns will remain constant for the foreseeable future, however will be periodically adjusted accordingly.

What regulations are you subject to?

We are operating pursuant to the state of Delaware's restrictions on large-cap loans and the 2016 revision of the SEC's Rule 504 of Regulation D.
